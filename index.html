About Penn Electric Racing

Penn Electric Racing is the student-run FSAE Team at the University of Pennsylvania.

Along the way to competing in FSAE Electric, students of all backgrounds gain practical experience working on a large-scale engineering project.

Penn Electric Racing took 1st place overall at FSAE Electric 2015 with our second formula car, REV-ONE.

We are currently hard at work designing and building REV-2.
